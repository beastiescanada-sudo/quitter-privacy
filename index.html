Privacy Policy for Quit-er

Effective date: March 17, 2026

Quit-er respects your privacy.

Quit-er is designed to help users track their progress while quitting vaping. The app stores your progress and settings locally on your device.

Information We Collect

Quit-er does not require you to create an account. Quit-er does not sell your personal information. Quit-er does not collect personal information for advertising purposes.

Local Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • your quit date
  • your goals and progress
  • your app preferences
  • your motivational quotes and settings

This information is used only so the app can function properly for you.

Notifications

Quit-er may request permission to send local notifications and reminders. These notifications are optional and can be turned off in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

Quit-er does not use third-party advertising services. Quit-er does not use third-party analytics services.

Data Sharing

Quit-er does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Quit-er is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted at this URL.
